Note: I have totally abused data-inset="true" on ALL the lists below as it just looks so much better!

Without data-inset="true" all standard, non-inset lists have a -15px margin to negate the 15px of padding on the content area to make lists fill to the edges of the screen. If you add other widgets above or below a list, the negative margin may make these elements overlap so you'll need to add additional spacing in your custom CSS.
